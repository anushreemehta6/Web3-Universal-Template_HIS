What is Web3?

Web3 represents the next evolution of the internet, shifting from centralized platforms to decentralized, trustless networks powered by blockchain technology. Unlike traditional Web2 applications controlled by corporations, Web3 enables peer-to-peer interactions, self-sovereign identities, and decentralized finance (DeFi).

Our courses cover the core principles of Web3, equipping you with the skills to build, secure, and innovate in this rapidly growing ecosystem.
